About

I am an aspiring web developer located in Pleasanton, CA. I graduated in 2019 with a Bachelor of Science in Physics from the University of California, Davis, where I gained experience programming in Python through research using cosmological simulations. I am currently learning web development skills at the UC Berkeley Coding Bootcamp. In my free time, I enjoy running, baking chocolaty desserts, and playing video games such as the Legend of Zelda and Animal Crossing.
